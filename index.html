I am a fifth year Ph.D. student at Princeton University. I work on control theory in the department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering and I am advised by Clancy Rowley. My research focuses on the control of plasma in tokamaks so I am also affiliated with the Princeton Plasma Physics Laboratory and I am co-advised by David Gates.
